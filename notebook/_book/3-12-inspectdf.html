3.12 inspectdf

Data exploration using inspectdf

  • descriptive statistics are performed seperatly for numeric and categroical + character
    • character treated as categorical
    • categorical dataframe only reports most common level - but provides a more detailed dataframe of all levels
  • inpsect_num and inpsect_cat have issues printing output due to nested tibbles
    • resolved by dropping hist and levels columns
  • global summary is not overly informative in comparison to dlookr
  • plots numeric distributions and categorical frequncies
    • categorical frequency plot is very nice
  • nativly supports comparisons between two dataframes.
